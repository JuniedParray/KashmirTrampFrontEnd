Valley Voyages Policy Statement

Introduction

At Valley Voyages, we are dedicated to providing exceptional travel experiences that are personalized, seamless, and enriching. This policy statement outlines our guiding principles, ensuring ethical, responsible, and customer-centric services that align with our mission to create memorable journeys.

Company Mission and Vision

Mission

To offer customizable and hassle-free travel itineraries that cater to diverse traveler needs, ensuring unforgettable experiences through high-quality service and innovative solutions.

Vision

To be a leading travel agency renowned for personalized travel planning, customer satisfaction, and responsible tourism.

Customer Service Policy

  • We prioritize customer satisfaction through responsive, transparent, and personalized service.
  • Our customer support team is available to assist with inquiries, bookings, modifications, and travel assistance.
  • We value customer feedback and continuously improve our services based on suggestions and reviews.

Booking and Payment Policy

  • Customers can book travel packages through our website, phone, or direct consultation.
  • Payment methods include credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and digital payment options.
  • A detailed invoice will be provided for each booking, outlining costs, inclusions, and policies.
  • To confirm a booking, customers are required to pay 10% of the total package amount in advance.
  • The remaining balance must be paid as per the payment schedule set by the company, which will be communicated at the time of booking.
  • Cancellation and refund policies vary by package and are clearly communicated at the time of booking.

Cancellation Policy

You Cancel Your Holiday

  • You, or any member of your party, may cancel their travel arrangements at any time. A written notification or an email from the person who made the booking must be received at our offices.
  • The applicable cancellation charges per person after confirming the package are as follows:
    • If cancellation is 30 days prior to arrival: 15% of the deposited amount towards the Tour Cost shall be refunded.
    • If cancellation is between 30 days to 15 days prior to arrival: 10% of the deposited amount towards the Tour Cost shall be refunded.
    • If cancellation is between 15 days to 07 days prior to arrival: No refund.
    • If cancellation is within 2 days prior to arrival: No Refund.
    • The above terms may vary based on specific cancellation charges applied by third-party service providers.

If We Change or Cancel Your Holiday

  • We plan travel arrangements in advance, and it is unlikely that we will have to make changes. However, we reserve the right to do so if necessary.
  • If any changes occur, we will notify you at the earliest possible date.
  • We also reserve the right to cancel your travel arrangements under any circumstances, with valid reasons assigned to you.
  • If we are unable to provide the booked travel arrangements, our liability shall be limited to the cost of your travel arrangements.

Privacy and Data Protection Policy

  • Valley Voyages is committed to protecting customer data and ensuring compliance with relevant privacy laws.
  • Personal information collected during booking is used solely for travel arrangements and service enhancements.
  • We do not share customer data with third parties without consent.

Ethical and Responsible Tourism Policy

  • We promote sustainable travel practices that respect local cultures, heritage, and environments.
  • Our itineraries encourage eco-friendly travel options, ethical wildlife interactions, and support for local communities.
  • We educate travelers on responsible tourism practices to minimize negative impacts.

Health and Safety Policy

  • Customer safety is our top priority. We partner with accredited transport providers, accommodations, and tour operators.
  • We provide travel insurance recommendations and safety guidelines for different destinations.
  • Emergency support and assistance are available for customers facing travel-related issues.

Employee Conduct and Ethics Policy

  • Our team upholds professionalism, integrity, and respect in all interactions.
  • Employees are trained to provide unbiased, informed, and ethical travel recommendations.
  • Discrimination, harassment, and unethical behavior are strictly prohibited.

Conflict Resolution Policy

  • Any customer grievances are addressed promptly and fairly.
  • We encourage open communication to resolve disputes amicably.
  • If necessary, mediation and legal avenues will be pursued to ensure just resolutions.

Compliance and Legal Policy

  • Valley Voyages adheres to local and international travel industry regulations.
  • We ensure compliance with visa, travel, and insurance requirements for various destinations.
  • All agreements with customers, suppliers, and partners are transparent and legally binding.

Environmental Responsibility Policy

  • We support eco-friendly travel options such as carbon-offset programs and sustainable accommodations.
  • Our operations strive to minimize waste, reduce carbon footprints, and promote responsible resource usage.
  • We collaborate with environmental organizations to advocate for sustainable tourism.

Policy Updates and Communication

  • This policy is reviewed periodically to align with industry best practices and legal requirements.
  • Updates are communicated via our website, customer emails, and direct notifications.
