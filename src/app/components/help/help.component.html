Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

General Information

Q: What is ValleyVoyages?

Travel & Accommodation

Q: What kind of accommodations do you provide?

A: We offer a range of accommodations, including luxury hotels, houseboats, budget hotels, and homestays.

Weather & Best Time to Visit

Q: When is the best time to visit Kashmir?

A: The best time to visit depends on your preference:

  • Spring (March-May): Blooming gardens and pleasant weather.
  • Summer (June-August): Perfect for sightseeing and adventure activities.
  • Autumn (September-November): Golden landscapes and serene surroundings.
  • Winter (December-February): Ideal for snow lovers and skiing in Gulmarg.
